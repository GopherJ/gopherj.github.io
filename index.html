Who am I

I'm a man, a coder, a vimer and a rustacean who can also write go...

Welcome to my website, my name is Cheng JIANG, I graduated from SHU and gained my Master degree at University of Troyes with a major in Network and Telecommunications.

Currently I'm working at Parallel Finance as Blockchain Lead. We are putting efforts to build the next generation of decentralized lending & staking protocol for Ethereum and Polkadot ecosystem.

Previously I was at Ubudu as Senior Full Stack Developer. I developed Ubudu's Real Time Location System, Social Distancing System (IOT solution for anti-covid19) and Embedded Device Management Platform.

In spare time, I do research and give talks in Zero Knowledge Proofs, Machine Learning, Distributed Systems and Blockchain. I'm active on GITHUB and contributing to privacy-scaling-explorations, scroll-tech, matter-labs, aave, parallel-finance, paraspace, casbin-rs, coc.nvim and many more...

This is my resume in english.

A Few Accomplishments

Computer science is a black hole, the closer you get, the more attracted you are.

Dompute

In this project, I helped doing research in Zk(E)VM, Hardware acceleration and ZK Coprocessor.

We participated ABCDE ZK Hackathon with a project called LambdaZkEVM, a ZK Coprocessor for expanding EVM's computational capability.

ParaSpace

In this project, we are developing an instant credit market for NFT and ERC20 assets.

We allow users to borrow and lend against Non-Fungible and Fungible tokens.

Parallel Finance

In this project, we are developing a decentralized lending & staking protocol for Polkadot ecosystem.

We allow users to earn "double interests" from staking and lending their tokens simultaneously.

McDonald's Drive Cloud Platform

Ubudu offers drive solution to McDonald's France and McDonald's USA.

In this project, I developed tunnel solution for intranet penetration and a supplementary cloud platform.

By using this platform, McDonald's is able to configure/upgrade/monitor its Drive-Ids remotely.

Social Distancing Platform

Ubudu designed its IOT Solution for fighting against covid19. This solution is based on BLE and UWB technology.

In this project, I'm responsible for the SDA Cloud Platform which gathers data from embedded devices and offers realtime analysis, visualization and hierarchical contact tracing to users.

This platform has served more than 100 companies all over the world.

Casbin-Rs

Casbin is an authorization library that supports access control models like ACL, RBAC, ABAC. I brought it to our company in 2019 and started to contribute to it.

We ported also Casbin from Go to Rust. At that time it was also the first full-featured authorization library in Rust ecosystem.
